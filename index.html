About

Elijah is an Atlanta-based UX/UI and Graphics Designer with experience across many different mediums and a wide variety of clients and industries.

He has gained unique experience working with a tech startup; having to wear many different hats, teaching himself and becoming proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite programs, and getting to be involved in other sides of the business.

Elijah's experience with McGriff has also benefited him in a completely different way. Having more responsibilites within a larger company has provided opportunities to work with clients from all kinds of industries from the food & restaurant industry, to construction, energy, and transportation.

Recently, Elijah has completed his UX/UI Design certificate with Georgia Tech Professional Learning. During this bootcamp he has learned the entire design process, became proficient in Figma, HTML, and CSS as well as other tools and softwares.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my work and I would love to connect!
